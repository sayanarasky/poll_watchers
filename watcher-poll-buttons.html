What changed & why

Activate toggle (top)
Removed → replaced by a status pill (Draft / Scheduled / Published)
The toggle mixed “is it saved?” with “is it live?” and gave no feedback on the current state. A read-only status pill answers both at a glance; the actual action moves to the footer buttons.
Save (final button)
Publish — or automatically Schedule when the start date is in the future, plus Save as draft
One contextual primary button always shows what will actually happen. Scheduling reuses the existing “Custom period → From” date, so no separate concept is needed. “Save as draft” lets you keep work without going live.
Briefly each day
Once a day + helper line
“Briefly” didn’t say briefly-relative-to-what. “Once a day” with a one-line explanation is unambiguous.
Display during the selected period
How often to show it
Shorter and describes the actual choice (frequency), not just a time window restatement.
Add rooms for poll publishing
Rooms to publish in
Less clunky; drops the noun-stacking (“poll publishing”).
One day
Single day
Reads less like a vague “someday” and more like “a single specific day”.
